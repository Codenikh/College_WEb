Admission

Admission Overview :

St. Andrews Institute of Technology and Management (SAITM), Gurgaon is one of the best Engineering colleges in India that provides professional degree programs. The college provides various courses at both undergraduate and postgraduate levels intending to create future leaders and engineers.

Students need to check the eligibility criteria before seeking BCA, BBA. MBA, B.tech, M.tech, MCA and D.Pharma admissions to St. Andrews Institute of Technology & Management.

Following are some of the details about the St. Andrews Institute of Technology and Management course admissions.
Admission Eligibility For SAITM Programs
SAITM offers admission under three major courses at the Undergraduate level:
B.Tech ( Bachelor of Technology) Eligibility for B.tech admission: Candidates seeking B-Tech admission should have Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects along with any one of these subjects - Chemistry/ Biotechnology/ Biology/ Technical Vocational Subject in 12th grade with minimum aggregate marks of 45%. Selection Process for B.Tech Admission: As per university norms and other competitive exams like JEE Main.

BCA ( Bachelor of Computer Applications) Eligibility: For seeking BCA admission, candidates should have passed class 12th with aggregate marks of 45% ( 42.75% marks for students under the SC/ST category of Haryana only) from a recognized board. Selection Proces for BCA Admission: As per university norms and other competitive exams.

BBA ( Bachelor of Business Application ) Eligibility: BBA admission eligibility criteria require candidates to have passed class 12th with minimum marks of 45% ( 42.75% marks for students under the SC/ST category of Haryana only) from a recognized board. Selection Process for BBA Admission: As per university norms and other competitive exams.

SAITM admission is offered under one course at Postgraduate Level:
MBA ( Master of Business Administration) MBA Admission Eligibility: Candidates must have scored minimum aggregate marks of 50% in their graduation from any field from a recognized board/university. Selection Process For MBA Admission: As per university norms and other competitive exams like CAT, MAT.

M.Tech ( Master of Technology) Eligibility for M.tech admission: Applicants should have a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering or a related field from a recognized university with a minimum percentage of 50%. Selection Process for M.Tech Admission: As per university norms.
